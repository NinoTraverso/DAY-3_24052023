Focaccia with Caramelised Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds wh this festive fcaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart
cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your favorite holiday starter!

FEATURED IN:
Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce


Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the toping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a parking knife if desired, though it's not
    necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia

    In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all
    the dough ingredients until the flower is fully absorbed. You don't need t actually knead the
    dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate
    the last flour...
